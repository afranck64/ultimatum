Letters Selection task!

In the following, you will see in the box containing the text "START" two possible letters: Either M or W. The letter M is associated with a gain of 10 USD cents and the letter W is associated with a lost of 5 USD cents. After a letter is presented, you will have 500 milliseconds (when the box is green) to click on the box to get the value corresponding to the last letter shown. By clicking only after seeing the letter M the bonus can be maximize. Your bonus on this task is the sum of the gains you collected. If your gain at the end is negative it will be reset to 0 USD cents.


Click on "START" to begin once you are ready.
