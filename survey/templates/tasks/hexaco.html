Questionnaire


Thank you for willing to participate! Your participation will help us greatly and we appreciate your time.


  • This will take most people between 3-6 minutes to complete.
  • You will find a series of statements about you.
  • Please read each statement and decide how much you agree or disagree with that statement.
Then indicate your response using the following scale:
  • 5 = strongly agree
  • 4 = agree
  • 3 = neutral (neither agree nor disagree)
  • 2 = disagree
  • 1 = strongly disagree.
